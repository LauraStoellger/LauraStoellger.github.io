ABOUT ME.

I study communication design at the HTW Berlin, while implementing small projects as a freelance graphic designer.
Before becoming a freelancer, I worked in the internet agency Pixup Media GmbH, where my main area of focus was designing online-shops.

I think, that design is a way to bring order into chaos and to express complex ideas more lucidly. I love straightforward design, that still leaves the observer space for interpretation. I am especially inspired by the works of Michael Schirner and Stefan Sagmeister.

“Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication” - Leonardo da Vinci
