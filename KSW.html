KAMPFSPORTWELT

Kampfsportwelt is a project I worked on during my time at Pixup Media GmbH. It is an online-shop for combat and martial arts equipment. We designed the Logo, as well as the entire shop.
This project was especially fun for me, because the design does not reflect my personal taste, which required me to put myself into the position of the target group.
